# Chai Pe Charcha — Admin Guide

Welcome to your online ordering system. This guide shows you how to run it day to
day. No technical knowledge needed — if you can use WhatsApp, you can use this.

---

## Your links

- **Your website (what customers see):** `https://your-domain.com`
- **Your admin panel (for you):** `https://your-domain.com/login`

Bookmark the admin link, or add it to your phone's home screen so it opens like an app.

---

## 1. Logging in

1. Open the admin link.
2. Enter your **email** and **password** (provided to you on handover).
3. **First thing to do:** change your password to something only you know — see
   "Change your password" at the end of this guide.

Keep your login private. Anyone with it can manage your orders and menu.

---

## 2. Managing orders (your daily job)

When a customer places an order, it appears in **Orders** automatically.

For each order you'll see the **customer's name, phone, address, items, and total**.

- Tap **✓ Accept** to confirm the order and start preparing it.
- Tap **✗ Reject** if you can't fulfil it.

The customer can see this status update on their "Track Order" page. The four cards
at the top show your counts: **Total, Pending, Accepted, Rejected**.

> Tip: keep the Orders page open during business hours so you see new orders as they arrive.

---

## 3. Managing your menu

Go to **Menu** in the sidebar. You'll see all your items as cards.

### Add a new item
1. Click **+ Add Item**.
2. Enter the **name** and **price**.
3. Choose a **category** (Karahi, Pizza, Chai, etc.).
4. Click to add a **photo** (a clear, well-lit picture works best).
5. Choose where it's available: **Dine In, Take Away, Delivery**.
6. Click **Save Item**. It appears on your website immediately.

### Edit an item
Click **✏️ Edit** on any card to change its name, price, photo, or settings.

### Delete an item
Click **🗑 Delete** to remove it permanently.

---

## 4. Availability — turn items on and off

On each menu item card:

- **✓ Active / Hidden** — Active means customers can see and order it. Hidden takes
  it off the website without deleting it (useful for seasonal items).
- **In stock / Sold out** — Mark something **Sold out** when you run out for the day.
  It stays visible but customers can't order it. Switch it back when you restock.

These change instantly on the website — great for "we're out of mutton today."

---

## 5. Photos

- Use a **clear, centred** photo of the dish — the system crops to a neat shape, so
  keep the food in the middle.
- Any normal phone photo works. The system automatically resizes and optimises it,
  so it loads fast for customers.

## Frequently asked questions

**A customer says they didn't get their order status.**
Statuses update when you tap Accept/Reject. Make sure you've done that.

**The website is slow the first time in the morning.**
If you're on a free hosting plan it may take a few seconds to "wake up" the first
visit of the day. Upgrading the hosting plan removes this. Ask your developer.

**I forgot my password.**
Contact your developer to reset it — see Support below.

**Can I add more staff logins?**
Yes — your developer can create additional admin accounts for your team.
